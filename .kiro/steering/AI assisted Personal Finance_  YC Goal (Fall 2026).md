# Clerkk: AI assisted Personal Finance:  YC Goal (Fall 2026\)

# “CFO for your life”

# 1\. Overview

Clerkk is your CFO for life: a mobile app that gives Canadian professionals instant clarity on their money today and confident next steps, without transaction tracking or CPA liability. We obsess over one thing: helping spreadsheet-haters make smarter moves, optimize taxes, shrink debt, hit goals, all dead simple one profile, three pillars (income, fixed expenses, debt), and a chat like texting a sharp buddy who knows CRA rules cold. We don't guess, we don't overpromise just reliable, actionable insight so you feel in control, not overwhelmed.

**Who It's For:**

This isn't for everyone. Clerk**k** targets Canadians earning one hundred thousand to two hundred fifty thousand dollars: tech, law, consulting pros with surplus to manage, not minimum wage folks or cashiers. If you're stretching every dollar for rent, this isn't your tool. We're built for complexity: bonuses, tax brackets, goals, not survival.

**What This App Isn't For:**

Clerkk isn't a budget tracker. No daily coffee logs, no receipt uploads. It doesn't scrape by on minimum wage or fix deep cashflow holes if you're broke. It won't pick investments, just nudges where to put money. It's not a CPA or advisor: think smart assistant, not licensed pro. Right now, it's one user only, no family or couple sharing. That could come later, but today it's your plan, not ours. If you need every penny tracked or joint setup, look elsewhere.

**North Star:**

Clerkk is your personal CFO for life not just tax help. It handles the full picture: surplus after debt and rent, monthly goal checks, one-off surprises, all recalculated so you stay ahead, not stressed. Taxes hook you; life keeps you.

# 2\. The Problem We Solve

Most apps either nag you about every five-dollar coffee or push generic robo-advice that misses your real life. Canadians earning one hundred thousand to two hundred fifty thousand dollars, tech, law, consulting, don’t want that. They want a trusted guide who does what their yearly Excel forecast does, tax-smart RRSP and TFSA plans, surplus splits, but doesn’t wait twelve months to update. Clerkk keeps their cashflow smooth, spotting trouble early, rerouting bonuses, adjusting for rent hikes so they never hit a wall.

# 3\. How Clerkk  Works

Clerkk acts as your CFO of your life, managing a single, living profile built from three things: income, fixed expenses, and debt. Set it up once, three minutes, done. Every month, you log your net paycheck, and Clerkk recalculates your surplus, spots tax wins, and suggests next moves. A bonus lands? It says, put three hundred to RRSP, save four hundred taxes. Rent goes up? It adjusts, maybe pause savings two months. The plan is clear, math-backed, always current. Follow it or not, your choice. Clerkk keeps the scoreboard: eighty two percent on track, five thousand seven hundred left this month. Ask anything, it answers fast, no judgment. You stay in charge, we just do the numbers.
